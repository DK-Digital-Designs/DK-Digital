Case Study

Ascend Elite Apparel

Modern sportswear and high-performance lifestyle brand.

GitHub Repo

The Problem

Apparel brands compete on aesthetics and speed. Ascend Elite needed a storefront that felt premium and loaded instantly to prevent cart abandonment on mobile devices.

The Solution

We engineered a "High-End" e-commerce experience using minimal dependencies. We built a custom cart drawer with 60fps animations and optimized the entire asset pipeline to ensure the site remains fast even with high-resolution imagery.

The Impact

A 25% decrease in page load time compared to their previous platform. The brand now has a digital storefront that matches the quality of their high-performance apparel.

The Stack

  • Vite
  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • Custom Animations
  • Image Pipeline

Technical Highlights

  • Custom cart drawer
  • Scroll-reveal logic
  • Mobile-first commerce
